Track 1: Within

Drawing Ourselves in Mini-Comics and Zines


Description

In this all-day workshop, conference attendees will learn how to design, format, and assemble personal mini-comics and zines. Manga editor Anne Ishii and letterer-illustrator Hannah K. Lee will guide participants in the process of delivering artist statements, creating visual themes, and choosing formats that will make their zines compelling to readers. Attendees will learn the zine-making process using templates that can be scaled to longer-form comics and books. We encourage everyone to participate in the exercise, and to draw from their experiences and personal narratives.
